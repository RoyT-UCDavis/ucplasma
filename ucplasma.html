Who We Are

Comprised of UC Davis entrepreneurs, corporate executives, and investors of various capacity, the PLASMA program consolidates the Davis startup community in a way that has never been seen before.

What We Do

We provide a small amount of seed capital, working spaces, and a plethora of networking opportunities. Our incubator is 12 weeks long and culminates with Demo Day where ventures pitch to a large audience of qualified investors for a top prize of $5,000. There are 2 cohorts per academic school year.

Our Philosophy

We are a community of UC Davis present, past, and future entrepreneurs dedicated to innovating the future.
At UC PLASMA, we build one another up, never tear each other down.
